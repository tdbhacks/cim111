Guestbook

I am a Computer Science major with a passion for drone photography. I got into photography during my freshman year, while taking an Introduction to Digital Filmmaking class.

Since then, I have taken several online classes as well as classes at the University of Miami, in order to improve my skills!

Everything I shoot is exported as DJI's native RAW format (.dmg), lightly edited on Adobe Photoshop CC to remove minor imperfections, and finally color graded on Adobe Lightroom CC using several different presets and manual adjustments.
